### 3. Technical SEO Files

**robots.txt:**
- Allows all crawlers access
- Specifies 1 second crawl delay
- Links to sitemap.xml
- Disallows sensitive directories

**sitemap.xml:**
- XML sitemap with 6 pages
- Includes URLs, lastmod dates, changefreq, and priority
- Priority: Home (1.0) → Portfolio (0.9) → Services (0.85) → About (0.8) → Contact (0.7) → Vision (0.6)
